GENERAL PEDIATRIC CARE

Our general pediatric care services provide comprehensive medical support for infants, children, and adolescents. We focus on routine health checkups, early diagnosis, and effective treatment of common childhood illnesses such as fever, infections, allergies, and growth concerns. Our pediatricians ensure personalized care in a warm, child-friendly environment that promotes overall physical and emotional well-being.

NEONATAL CARE

Our neonatal care unit is dedicated to providing specialized medical care for newborn babies, including premature and low-birth-weight infants. With advanced monitoring systems and experienced neonatal specialists, we ensure constant observation, temperature regulation, nutritional support, and timely medical intervention during the most critical early days of life.

IMMUNIZATION SERVICES

We offer complete immunization services following nationally and internationally recommended vaccination schedules. Our vaccination programs help protect children from serious and preventable diseases while strengthening their immune system. Parents are guided with proper counseling, reminders, and post-vaccination care for maximum safety and effectiveness.

CHILD GROWTH & DEVELOPMENT

Monitoring a child’s growth and development is essential for long-term health. Our specialists regularly assess physical growth, cognitive development, speech, and emotional behavior to identify potential delays at an early stage. Early intervention and parental guidance help ensure children achieve healthy developmental milestones.

NUTRITION & DIET COUNSELLING

Proper nutrition plays a vital role in a child’s growth and immunity. Our nutrition and diet counselling services provide personalized diet plans based on age, growth needs, and medical conditions. We guide parents on healthy eating habits, balanced diets, and nutritional support to promote strong immunity and optimal development.

PAEDIATRIC EMERGENCY CARE

Our pediatric emergency care services operate round-the-clock to provide immediate medical attention during critical situations. Equipped with trained pediatric specialists and child-friendly emergency facilities, we ensure rapid diagnosis and prompt treatment for injuries, breathing problems, infections, and other emergencies, offering reassurance to both children and parents.

Trusted healthcare with advanced technology and compassionate care.
