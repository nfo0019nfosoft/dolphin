What is Assisted Reproductive Technology?

Nurturing Parenthood,
One Step Closer

Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART) refers to medical procedures that help individuals or couples conceive when natural conception is difficult or impossible. ART involves manipulating eggs, sperm, or embryos outside the human body and transferring embryos into the uterus to achieve pregnancy.

Assisted reproductive technology can help with both male and female infertility, repeated pregnancy loss, or some genetic problems. Common treatments include IVF, IUI, ICSI, donor programs, freezing eggs, sperm, or embryos, and genetic testing before pregnancy.

How ART Changes the Fertility Journey.

Benefits That Bring You Closer to Parenthood

  • Provides solutions for infertility in men, women, and couples.
  • Offers fertility preservation options through cryopreservation.
  • Allows genetic screening for healthier pregnancies.
  • Increases pregnancy and live birth rates.
  • ART increases the success rate of conception by up to 60% for many couples.

Before beginning Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART), our fertility specialists in Hyderabad perform a complete hormonal and ultrasound evaluation.

Considerations That Matter in Parenthood

  • Higher likelihood of multiple pregnancies.
  • Risk of Ovarian Hyperstimulation Syndrome (OHSS) in women undergoing stimulation.
  • Emotional and financial challenges during treatment.
  • Success rates vary depending on age, fertility factors, and ART technique used.
