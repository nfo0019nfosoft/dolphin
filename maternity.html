ANTENATAL CARE (PRENATAL CARE)

Our antenatal care services provide comprehensive medical support throughout pregnancy to ensure the health and safety of both mother and baby. Regular checkups, ultrasound scans, blood tests, and monitoring of fetal growth help detect potential risks early. Personalized guidance on nutrition, lifestyle, and pregnancy care is provided to ensure a smooth and healthy pregnancy journey.

HIGH-RISK PREGNANCY CARE

We offer specialized care for high-risk pregnancies with conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, multiple pregnancies, or previous pregnancy complications. Our experienced obstetric team ensures continuous monitoring, advanced diagnostics, and timely medical interventions to minimize risks and promote positive outcomes for both mother and baby.

NORMAL & CESAREAN DELIVERY

Our maternity unit supports both normal vaginal deliveries and cesarean sections in a safe, hygienic, and well-equipped environment. Skilled obstetricians, anesthetists, and nursing staff work together to ensure smooth labor, pain management, and emergency readiness, prioritizing the comfort and safety of the mother and newborn.

LABOUR PAIN MANAGEMENT

We provide effective labour pain management options tailored to the needs of each mother. From breathing techniques and supportive care to medical pain relief methods, our team ensures a comfortable and reassuring birthing experience while closely monitoring the progress of labor.

POSTNATAL CARE

Postnatal care focuses on the physical and emotional recovery of the mother after childbirth. Our services include monitoring maternal health, wound care, breastfeeding support, newborn care guidance, and emotional well-being support to help mothers transition smoothly into motherhood.

BREASTFEEDING & LACTATION SUPPORT

Our lactation support services help new mothers initiate and maintain successful breastfeeding. Certified lactation consultants provide guidance on proper feeding techniques, positioning, milk supply management, and solutions to common breastfeeding challenges, ensuring optimal nutrition and bonding for mother and baby.
